Why Your Support Matters

Ukraine continues to face immense humanitarian, social, and legal challenges as a result of the ongoing full‑scale invasion. Communities struggle with disrupted infrastructure, displacement, and the long-term consequences of war. Your support helps us stand with Ukrainians in a way that is practical, principled, and grounded in real needs.

WSUA directs donor support toward three core areas:

  • Humanitarian assistance — providing essential items such as generators and power equipment to help communities endure blackouts and infrastructure attacks.
  • Support for Ukrainian organizations — partnering with trusted groups on the ground to strengthen local resilience and community programs.
  • Legal research and advocacy — advancing rigorous work on reparations, restitution, and mass claims mechanisms, including a comparative research paper and an accessible layperson’s guide.

Every contribution — large or small — directly strengthens these efforts and helps build a foundation for justice, recovery, and long-term dignity for Ukraine.
