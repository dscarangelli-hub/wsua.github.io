Our Mission

WSUA mobilizes communities across the Western United States to support Ukraine through transparent aid, cultural solidarity, and sustained partnership. We believe solidarity is a living bond — one built through action, trust, and shared purpose.

What We Do

Humanitarian Aid

Food, hygiene kits, winter gear, and essential supplies for displaced families across Ukraine.

Medical Support

Trauma kits, first‑aid supplies, and assistance for clinics serving high‑need communities.

Education & Youth Programs

Support for schools, youth centers, and community rebuilding initiatives.

Community Resilience

Micro‑grants and local partnerships that strengthen long‑term recovery and cultural connection.
