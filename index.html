Our Mission

WSUA mobilizes communities across the Western United States to support Ukraine through transparent aid, cultural solidarity, and sustained partnership. We believe solidarity is a living bond — one built through action, trust, and shared purpose.

What We Do

Humanitarian Aid

Emergency relief, food, hygiene kits, winter gear, and essential supplies for families across Ukraine.

Reconstruction

Supporting rebuilding efforts for homes, schools, clinics, and community infrastructure damaged by war.

Community Resilience

Micro‑grants, local partnerships, and long‑term recovery initiatives that strengthen Ukrainian communities.

Diaspora Support

Connecting and empowering Ukrainian diaspora communities across the Western United States.
