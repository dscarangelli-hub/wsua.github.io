Our Mission

WSUA mobilizes communities across the Western United States to support Ukraine through humanitarian aid, legal research, and justice‑focused advocacy. We work to protect the rights of displaced Ukrainians, strengthen accountability efforts, and build the foundations for long‑term recovery and restitution.

What We Do

Humanitarian Aid

Emergency relief, medical supplies, and direct support for displaced families.

Reconstruction

Legal and logistical research supporting the rebuilding of homes and infrastructure.

Community Resilience

Micro‑grants, legal navigation assistance, and long‑term recovery programs.

Diaspora Support

Legal guidance and empowerment for Ukrainians across the Western U.S.
