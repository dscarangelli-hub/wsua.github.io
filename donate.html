Where Your Donation Goes

  • Humanitarian Aid: Emergency relief, medical supplies, and direct support for displaced families.
  • Legal Research & Advocacy: Documentation, restitution frameworks, and transitional justice work.
  • Community Resilience: Micro‑grants, legal navigation assistance, and long‑term recovery programs.
  • Operational Transparency: WSUA maintains strict financial accountability and publishes regular reports.

For full financial transparency, visit our Transparency Page.
