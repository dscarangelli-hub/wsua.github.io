Our Programs

Building resilience, relief, and long‑term recovery.

How We Support Ukraine

WSUA focuses on four core program areas designed to meet urgent needs while strengthening long‑term community resilience. Each program is built in partnership with trusted organizations in Ukraine and diaspora communities across the Western United States.

Humanitarian Aid

Emergency relief including food, hygiene kits, medical supplies, winter clothing, and essential support for displaced families and frontline communities.

Reconstruction

Supporting the rebuilding of homes, schools, clinics, and community infrastructure damaged or destroyed by war, with a focus on long‑term recovery.

Community Resilience

Micro‑grants, local partnerships, and community‑driven initiatives that strengthen Ukrainian resilience and empower local leadership.

Diaspora Support

Connecting and empowering Ukrainian diaspora communities across the Western United States through cultural events, advocacy, and community‑building.
