Claims Guidance

Understanding future restitution and claims processes for Ukraine.

About This Page

This page provides accessible guidance on potential future claims processes related to war‑related losses, displacement, and property destruction. While no unified claims mechanism exists yet, WSUA is preparing communities by offering clear, research‑based information.

How to Prepare for Future Claims

  1. Collect and preserve all documents related to property ownership or loss.
  2. Record dates, locations, and circumstances of damage or displacement.
  3. Gather photographs, videos, or witness statements when possible.
  4. Store digital copies in multiple secure locations.
  5. Stay informed about emerging legal frameworks and international mechanisms.
