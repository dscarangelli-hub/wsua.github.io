Diaspora Support

Legal guidance and community empowerment across the Western U.S.

Supporting Ukrainians Abroad

WSUA provides resources, legal navigation assistance, and community support for Ukrainians living across the Western United States. Our goal is to strengthen diaspora networks and ensure displaced individuals have access to the tools they need.

Our Diaspora Support Includes

  • Legal guidance on documentation, immigration, and rights
  • Community events and cultural programming
  • Support networks for newly arrived families
  • Workshops on restitution, claims, and transitional justice

Why This Matters

Diaspora communities play a vital role in Ukraine’s long‑term recovery. By empowering Ukrainians abroad with legal knowledge and community support, we strengthen both individual resilience and collective advocacy for justice.
